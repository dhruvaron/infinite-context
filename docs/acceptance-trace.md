Audit date: 2026-07-14<br>

Release verdict: **NOT ACCEPTED**

The schema-17 hardening tree contains source for the planned V1 surface, including normalized bounded confirmation proposals, generation-isolated embeddings, generation-plus-repair-token CAS projection repair, and fail-closed deletion recovery, but this revision is **not release-verified**. The installed dependency state is unusable, dependency restoration was not authorized during this audit, and typecheck, lint, contract drift, unit/integration tests, production build, native ingestion, macOS sandbox, Playwright, load, and evaluation commands were not rerun. The checked-in reports below are historical pre-schema-17 evidence, not results for the current tree.

The release is also missing its required empirical claim package: there is no paid complete controlled benchmark, matched ChatGPT/Codex capture, normal-provider latency artifact, or frozen-revision fresh-checkout/Keychain record; the deterministic 10,000-message fixture misses the 15% relative-accuracy target. Recorded provider/API spend remains **USD 0.00**. No paid or normal-provider call was made.

Status vocabulary is deliberately strict:

- **verified** — the current revision itself has directly relevant, reproducible evidence.
- **historically observed** — a result was recorded on an earlier uncommitted/pre-hardening tree and is retained with its original evidence class.
- **source present** — implementation is visible in the current tree, but current-revision execution evidence is absent.
- **partial** — implementation or historical evidence exists, but current-revision, release-bound, or external proof is incomplete.
- **blocked** — required external/live evidence is absent, or a frozen gate is known to miss.

Deterministic fixtures, mock-provider journeys, and local load measurements are never promoted to live-model or competitor evidence. The checked-in artifacts identify an uncommitted historical workspace revision and must be regenerated from the schema-17 frozen release revision. Source-only syntax/hygiene checks and raw migration smoke, when recorded, are not substitutes for typecheck, tests, build, E2E, load, or evaluation suites.

### Intentional web-evidence boundary

The former GAP-MEM-05 wording assumed that a provider citation span was equivalent to locally fetched page text. It is not. V1 persists the answer, citation title/URL/span, timestamps, freshness class, and provenance, but does not claim possession of the cited page body unless separate exact source bytes were actually ingested. The compiler may attribute the answer to the citation metadata; it must not create exact page-text claims from unseen content. `apps/api/src/orchestrator-web.test.ts` locks this conservative behavior.

## Release decision

The repository may be demonstrated as schema-17 prototype source alongside clearly labeled historical no-cost artifacts: a complete deterministic 10,000-message production-path diagnostic and a schema-11 100,000-event storage/search run. The current revision may not be called feature-complete, release-validated, or represented by those artifacts. It may not be described as empirically superior to ChatGPT, Codex, rolling compaction, or flat retrieval, and it may not claim live latency targets, full-compiler O(delta) behavior, or proven scalability to thousands of genuine durable facts. Recorded provider spend is USD 0.00. The verdict remains **NOT ACCEPTED** until current-revision validation, external evidence, and scale blockers are closed or explicitly waived in the binding plan.
